Bring Selah to your whole church.

Selah for Church puts everyone -- pastors, staff, small-group leaders, and members -- on one shared setup instead of separate individual accounts. A church admin manages seats and a roster from one dashboard; everyone gets their own private study conversations underneath it.

How it works

Leader seats
For pastors, staff, and teaching leaders. Full access for sermon prep, teaching, and deep study, plus roster and billing admin for the first leader.
Member seats
For congregation members. Personal study access under the church's umbrella, added once your church has an active leader plan.
One shared dashboard
A church admin adds and removes people, sees seat usage, and manages billing in one place -- no one juggling separate personal subscriptions.
Private by person
Everyone's own conversations and study history stay their own. The shared setup is for seats and billing, not for reading anyone's conversations.
