Selah for Churches in Action

Leadership
Pastors, leaders, teachers and board members are perfect candidates for leadership seats. They will receive full access and will also have access to the session recap which can assist with a written session summary, the source material included, citations from this material and discussion questions, if desired. Different sessions can be recalled at any time for continuing study.
Membership
Members have access to the same underlying biblical material and resources, just without the session recall features. Their sessions also remain persistent for reentry.
Selah Dashboard
Billing details, adding members, reassigning roles, and roster adjustments are all consolidated in one place for administrative ease of use. Church planning tools can also be accessed here.
Privacy
Each leader or member can be assured no one else in the church has access to their private conversations. Non-personal, aggregated topic information may be collected for church planning purposes.
